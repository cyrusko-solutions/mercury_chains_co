Welcome to Mercury Chains Co

Mercury Chains Co is the contemporary expression of a legacy that began in 1940 with Jagmohan Das & Co., one of North India’s most trusted names in the gold bullion trade. For over eight decades, our family has served as quiet custodians of purity, precision, and trust in precious metals.

What began as a wholesale bullion enterprise supplying high-purity gold to jewellers, investors, and institutions has now evolved with the same integrity and craftsmanship into a more personal offering.

In 2023, we launched Mercury Chains Co, a fine jewellery house specialising in elegant gold chains, bracelets, and timeless ornaments, crafted entirely in-house. By removing intermediaries, we offer our patrons the advantage of refined quality at unmatched value, rooted in our deep knowledge of metal, pricing, and detail.

Each piece is BIS hallmarked and HUID certified, and presented with full documentation to ensure transparency, traceability, and peace of mind.

Whether you are purchasing for beauty, tradition, or investment, our gold is exactly what it promises to be.

About

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted and transparent jewellery chain brand by offering hallmark-certified, affordable, and high-quality gold and silver bullion and gold ornaments directly from our in-house manufacturing unit to consumers across the nation.

Our Mission

  • To maintain transparency in every transaction and ensure each product is HUID-verified and certified.
  • To bridge the gap between the manufacturer and end consumer by eliminating middlemen.
  • To offer premium gold chains, bracelets, and other jewellery at the most reasonable making charges starting at 3%.
  • To expand our presence from Lucknow to various cities across India while delivering PAN India with proper billing and documentation.
  • To uphold our legacy and trust built since 1940 under the esteemed trade name Jagmohan Das & Co.

Our Collection

  • Stylish lightweight gold jewellery, including chains under ₹20,000 designed for everyday elegance
  • Gold bullion and coins, available in 1g to 1kg denominations in both bar and coin forms, with custom weights available on request

With nationwide delivery, secure packaging, and a personalised customer experience, Mercury Chains Co invites you to discover gold as it was meant to be — honest, timeless, and beautifully made.

Privacy Policy (WhatsApp Cloud API & General)

Overview

Mercury Chains Co uses WhatsApp Cloud API (provided by Meta/WhatsApp) to send and receive messages for customer support, order updates, booking confirmations, user verification, and related communications. This policy explains what data we collect via WhatsApp integrations, why we collect it, how we store and share it, and your rights.

Data we collect

  • Phone number you provide when you contact us via WhatsApp.
  • Message content you send or receive (text, media you choose to upload like images or documents).
  • Message metadata (timestamps, message IDs) and any profile information you share (name, profile photo) available via WhatsApp.
  • Order or booking details, verification details, or activation data if you provide them through WhatsApp.

How we use your data

  • To respond to enquiries, provide customer support, and manage orders or bookings.
  • To send verification codes, verification confirmations, and activation confirmations.
  • To send transactional messages such as booking confirmations, delivery updates, payment reminders, and order status notifications.
  • To maintain records necessary for billing, dispute resolution, and fraud prevention.

WhatsApp Cloud API & Meta

We use the WhatsApp Cloud API, a service run by Meta (WhatsApp). When you message us on WhatsApp, certain data is processed by Meta’s systems. Meta's privacy policies and terms apply to that processing — please see WhatsApp/Meta documentation for details. We do not control Meta's independent processing of data; we only control how we use the data we receive from the API.

WhatsApp API Use Cases

We use WhatsApp Cloud API strictly for transactional and service-related purposes, including:

  • User Verification: Sending OTP or verification codes to confirm user identity.
  • Verification Confirmation: Confirming successful verification of your WhatsApp number or account.
  • Activation Confirmation: Notifying you when your account, membership, or service is activated.
  • Order Placement Notifications: Sending order confirmation to customers and notifying hosts/vendors when an order is placed.
  • Order Status Updates: Sending updates related to order processing, approval, rejection, dispatch, delivery, or any required user action.

Data sharing and processors

We may share data with:

  • Service providers who help deliver our service (e.g., payment processors, delivery partners) — only when necessary and under contract.

Retention

We retain WhatsApp messages and related records only as long as necessary to provide our services, to comply with legal obligations, or to resolve disputes — typically up to 2 years unless a different retention period is required by law or our operational needs. You can request deletion as described below.

We take reasonable measures to protect the data we store, including access controls and encrypted backups. However, no system is perfectly secure; avoid sending extremely sensitive personal data over WhatsApp if possible.
