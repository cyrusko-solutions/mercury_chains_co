Welcome to Mercury Chains Co

Mercury Chains Co is the contemporary expression of a legacy that began in 1940 with Jagmohan Das & Co., one of North India’s most trusted names in the gold bullion trade. For over eight decades, our family has served as quiet custodians of purity, precision, and trust in precious metals.

What began as a wholesale bullion enterprise supplying high-purity gold to jewellers, investors, and institutions has now evolved with the same integrity and craftsmanship into a more personal offering.

In 2023, we launched Mercury Chains Co, a fine jewellery house specialising in elegant gold chains, bracelets, and timeless ornaments, crafted entirely in-house. By removing intermediaries, we offer our patrons the advantage of refined quality at unmatched value, rooted in our deep knowledge of metal, pricing, and detail.

Each piece is BIS hallmarked and HUID certified, and presented with full documentation to ensure transparency, traceability, and peace of mind.

Whether you are purchasing for beauty, tradition, or investment, our gold is exactly what it promises to be.

About

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted and transparent jewellery chain brand by offering hallmark-certified, affordable, and high-quality gold and silver bullion and gold ornaments directly from our in-house manufacturing unit to consumers across the nation.

Our Mission

  • To maintain transparency in every transaction and ensure each product is HUID-verified and certified.
  • To bridge the gap between the manufacturer and end consumer by eliminating middlemen.
  • To offer premium gold chains, bracelets, and other jewellery at the most reasonable making charges starting at 3%.
  • To expand our presence from Lucknow to various cities across India while delivering PAN India with proper billing and documentation.
  • To uphold our legacy and trust built since 1940 under the esteemed trade name Jagmohan Das & Co.

Our Collection

  • Stylish lightweight gold jewellery, including chains under ₹20,000 designed for everyday elegance
  • Gold bullion and coins, available in 1g to 1kg denominations in both bar and coin forms, with custom weights available on request

With nationwide delivery, secure packaging, and a personalised customer experience, Mercury Chains Co invites you to discover gold as it was meant to be — honest, timeless, and beautifully made.

Privacy Policy (Application & WhatsApp Communication)

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts

  • To process orders, bookings, and related transactions
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To ensure application security and prevent misuse

WhatsApp Communication

We use WhatsApp (via WhatsApp Cloud API provided by Meta) strictly for transactional and service-related communication such as:

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information (Name, Phone Number, Email) with any third-party services for marketing or commercial purposes.

Your data is used strictly for internal application operations.

Data Security

While we take strong precautions, no digital system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain user information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. WhatsApp communication data is not retained.
